Current Investigation

Operation: Walkies Deployment

Case File #5104

Departure Delayed
Location Front Hallway
Lead Hooman Dad
Department Walkies & Outdoor Operations
Status Harness Temporarily Confiscated
Watch on YouTube

CEO’s Notes

Dad announced the commencement of walkies and attempted to begin the standard harness-fitting procedure. Management responded with immediate and overwhelming enthusiasm.

Rather than remaining stationary, the CEO initiated a series of high-speed hops, spins and evasive manoeuvres. The harness was then seized and removed from Dad’s possession without authorisation.

This behaviour occurs during nearly every scheduled walkies deployment. Although it creates a minor delay, it also demonstrates exceptional morale, strong commitment to outdoor operations and an admirable refusal to approach routine tasks routinely.

Official Management Verdict

Walkies remain fully approved. Dad is advised to allow additional preparation time and to maintain a secure grip on all harness-related equipment until the CEO has completed the required pre-departure celebrations.
