Operation: Catch Dad

Case File #4827

Evidence Inconclusive
Location Local Playground
Lead Hooman Dad
Department Recreation & Zoomies
Status Fun Was Had by All
Watch on YouTube

CEO’s Notes

Dad announced that today’s assignment was to film a simple playground adventure. Management remained cautiously optimistic.

Three attempts later, the official footage included excellent views of the sky, several close inspections of the grass, and one dramatic camera collapse. Technical performance was inconsistent.

Fortunately, the final attempt produced sufficient evidence that everyone had a brilliant time, zoomies were successfully completed, and workplace morale received a significant boost.

Official Management Verdict

Playground excursions are approved for future team-building activities. Dad has been instructed to complete additional camera operator training before the next assignment.
