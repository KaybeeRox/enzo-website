Petface Cyril the Squirrel Review

A soft, squeaky squirrel designed for cuddling, carrying and gentle play. Currently one of Enzo's favourite toys, though its long-term survival remains under active review.

4/5 Paws

Cyril the Squirrel is a soft plush toy with a built-in squeaker, making it ideal for carrying, cuddling and interactive play.

Enzo took to it immediately and it has quickly become one of his favourite toys. While it feels better made than many budget plush toys, experience suggests that no stuffed animal survives forever in this office.

❤ What Enzo Loved

  • Soft enough to cuddle after a busy day of supervising the office.
  • The built-in squeaker keeps play sessions lively and entertaining.
  • Although quite large for an 8 kg cockapoo, Enzo has no trouble carrying it, shaking it enthusiastically, or proudly showing it off.
  • Better stitched and more substantial than many cheaper plush toys we've tested.
  • It became one of Enzo's favourite toys almost immediately and is still in regular rotation.

! Things to Know

  • Designed for cuddling, gentle chewing and interactive play.
  • Suitable for dogs of all breed sizes.
  • Includes a built-in squeaker.
  • Like all plush toys, it will eventually become vulnerable to heavy chewing.
  • Petface toys generally seem to last longer than many inexpensive alternatives we've tried.
  • Always supervise play and replace the toy if loose stuffing or damaged fabric becomes a hazard.

♛ CEO’s Final Verdict

Cyril the Squirrel has made an excellent first impression.

It is soft, well made and clearly designed for dogs who enjoy carrying their favourite toy everywhere they go. Enzo was interested from the moment it appeared and continues to choose it regularly.

Realistically, every plush toy eventually reaches the end of its career. The question isn't if—it is when. Based on previous Petface toys, however, we expect Cyril to remain employed longer than many cheaper alternatives.

Based on Enzo's enthusiasm, the quality of construction and our experience with Petface plush toys, Cyril the Squirrel earns a solid recommendation for dogs who enjoy soft toys.
