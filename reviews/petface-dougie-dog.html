Petface Dougie Dog Review

A long, soft squeaky sausage dog designed for carrying, cuddling and enthusiastic play. Sadly, Dougie's career was memorable but unexpectedly brief.

3.5/5 Paws

Quick Verdict

Dougie Dog is a lightweight plush toy with a soft cord fleece texture and built-in squeaker, making it perfect for carrying, shaking and cuddling.

Enzo absolutely loved it from the moment it arrived. Unfortunately, despite its strong construction claims, Dougie's employment ended sooner than expected after suffering catastrophic workplace damage.

❤ What Enzo Loved

  • The long body made it easy to carry around the house.
  • Perfect size and shape for vigorous squeaking sessions.
  • The soft cord fabric made it equally suitable for cuddling between play sessions.
  • Lightweight enough for throwing, shaking and proudly parading around the office.
  • It quickly became a favourite while it lasted.

! Things to Know

  • Designed for puppies and smaller dogs.
  • Soft cord fleece exterior with an internal squeaker.
  • Excellent for carrying, gentle play and indoor games.
  • Despite its design, our example didn't last as long as other Petface plush toys we've tested.
  • As with all plush toys, supervise play and replace the toy if damaged.

♛ CEO’s Final Verdict

Dougie Dog had all the ingredients of a great plush toy.

Enzo loved carrying it, squeaking it and generally making sure everyone in the office knew he owned it.

Unfortunately, it didn't survive as long as we expected. Compared with some of Petface's other plush toys, Dougie reached retirement much sooner than anticipated.

A fun toy that Enzo genuinely enjoyed, but one that ultimately falls short on durability. A respectable 3.5-paw review from the Enzo Has Notes Toy Testing Department.
