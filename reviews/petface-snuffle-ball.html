Petface Snuffle Ball Review

A compact snuffle feeder that turns treat time into an engaging nose-work activity while providing valuable mental enrichment.

5/5 Paws

Quick Verdict

The Petface Snuffle Ball is a compact enrichment feeder that encourages dogs to sniff, search and problem-solve while hunting for hidden treats.

It has become one of Enzo's favourite enrichment activities. Unlike larger snuffle mats, it is small enough to store easily while still providing plenty of entertainment and mental stimulation.

❤ What Enzo Loved

  • Hunting for treats is even more exciting than simply being handed one.
  • The compact design takes up far less room than a traditional snuffle mat while providing the same engaging challenge.
  • Encourages natural sniffing and foraging behaviours that keep curious dogs mentally engaged.
  • Makes an excellent distraction when the humans need to leave the house, helping turn departures into a more positive experience.
  • Durable enough for regular enrichment sessions and now part of Enzo's daily routine.

! Things to Know

  • Suitable for dogs of all sizes.
  • Designed to encourage sniffing, searching and problem solving.
  • Hide dry treats or kibble between the fabric folds for maximum engagement.
  • Smaller than many traditional snuffle mats, making it easier to store and transport.
  • Like any fabric enrichment toy, it should be used under supervision and checked regularly for wear.
  • Particularly useful for energetic or intelligent breeds that benefit from extra mental stimulation.

♛ CEO’s Final Verdict

This is one of those products that quietly becomes part of your everyday routine.

Enzo loves using his nose to hunt for treats, and this snuffle ball keeps him happily occupied while giving his brain a proper workout.

Its compact design delivers the same enrichment as much larger snuffle mats without taking over the living room, making it easier to store and use every day.

Whether it's for everyday enrichment or giving Enzo something positive to focus on before we leave the house, this has become one of our favourite enrichment feeders. It earns a full five-paw recommendation.
