Petface Nobbly Crocodile Review

A tough, textured crocodile toy assessed after more than a year of chewing, carrying, tossing, and determined attempts at executive-level destruction.

5/5 Paws

Quick Verdict

The Petface Nobbly Crocodile is a bright plush dog toy made with a durable fabric body and tough rubber nodules for added chewing texture.

Enzo has been playing with his for more than a year. Although it now carries several well-earned battle scars, it remains recognisable, largely intact, and still part of his regular toy rotation.

❤ What Enzo Loved

  • Considerably tougher than the average soft or plush dog toy.
  • The rubber nodules provide an interesting texture for chewing and gnawing.
  • Its long shape makes it easy to carry, shake, throw, and retrieve.
  • Suitable for both independent play and interactive games with a human.
  • It has survived more than a year of regular use by a dog who takes toy testing very seriously.

! Things to Know

  • Petface describes the crocodile as a seriously strong plush toy made with tough rubber nodules.
  • The textured surface is designed to add interest during chewing and play.
  • It works well for tossing, carrying, and supervised interactive games.
  • It is strong, but it is not indestructible.
  • After prolonged use, the fabric and extremities may begin to fray or open.
  • Supervise play and examine the toy regularly for loose fabric, stuffing, or rubber pieces.

♛ CEO’s Final Verdict

The Petface Nobbly Crocodile has outlasted many of the toys submitted to Enzo’s testing department.

It is not completely undamaged. There is visible fraying and wear from more than a year of chewing, but the main body has remained intact and Enzo continues to play with it.

The combination of a sturdy fabric body, rubber chewing nodules, and an easy-to-carry shape makes it useful for several different types of play.

Based on its exceptional longevity and Enzo’s continued interest, the Petface Nobbly Crocodile passes the Enzo Has Notes quality review with an unusually strong durability record.
